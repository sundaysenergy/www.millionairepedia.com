Wealth File #2

Rich people play the money game to win.
Poor people play the money game not to lose.

Poor people usually try to get their bills paid and put food on the table. That's their goal, and they (usually) just make it... and nothing more.

Middle class people want to be comfortable and have a few toys. If you're only "comfortable", you don't have to stay up late worrying about how to pay the bills - you can even go out and enjoy yourself once in a while. But you have to think about what you can afford all the time, and only enjoy things that fall into that category. You can't afford to even think about what you'd do if you really had money.

Rich people, on the other hand, do what they most want to do. The entire world is their playground. Hunting in Botswana? Sure, sounds like fun. Scuba diving off the coast of Saudi Arabia? Why not? The coral's beautiful. In the "millionaire book of options," nothing is off the table.

If you're shooting for "comfortable", you'll never be anything more. But if you attempt to be wealthy, you'll be comfortable in style, anywhere you want to be. If you train for a marathon, you might not cross the finish line. But you'll go miles farther - and faster - than you ever could have without the training.

Allow yourself to dream without boundaries. Soon you will be ready to get rid of the financial freedom books gathering dust on your shelves - you have already started to write your own millionaire mind book, as well as live it. As we continue to explore the "wealth files" that will underlie successful thinking and millionaire secrets, you will begin to see wealth come into your life so get ready!
