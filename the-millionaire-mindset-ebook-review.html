The Millionaire Mindset eBook Review

Vene and Grubisa’s The Millionaire Mindset: How to Tap Real Wealth from Within is a book/guide about self-discovery, development, and personal success. It offers a positive philosophy, uplifting messages, and techniques on self-conditioning; while at the same time throwing in practical and financial applications to these messages. It mixes up business and spirituality in one glass - proving that the two are a compatible (and perhaps, the only) match. It’s both a self-help and business book in one.

The Millionaire Mindset Home Page

Seven Figure Ability:

From a money-making perspective, this book does not really tackle making money per se. The focus is more on redirecting energies to pave a path to success (which can in turn generate money in the process).

Its lessons on investment, however, are worth a gem. It sees things from a small-time investor’s perspective and gives tips on how to get around the big-time competitors no matter how small the capital is.

It is brutally honest about the way the business world works. All throughout the book, it’s either breaking certain myths and misconceptions that stop people from getting more successful, or it’s stating how to get around this dog-eat-dog world and come out a success with still your honor and sense of morality intact.

Ease of Learning:

Originally written in Slovene, the translators made it a point to make the translation
(and the transition from one culture to another) smooth enough. Some of the concepts were refined for the American audience.

Therefore, it is a guaranteed easy read. The concepts are pretty graspable even for someone without previous knowledge of psychology and business.

Customer Service:

The Millionaire Mindset eBook offers online support through a ticket-based helpdesk system.

Overall:

Overall, this book-guide is worth looking at; especially for a first book on business and self-help. It is great for beginning and experienced investors. The book is an excellent combination for any money-making venture a person decides to get into or wants to prepare for. The bonuses that come with the book are very useful.
